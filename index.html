Welcome to My Sports World

Sports have always been a big part of my life. Whether it’s shooting hoops, playing soccer on the field, or watching highlight reels late at night, I love the energy, focus, and teamwork that sports bring.

This website is a small snapshot of the sports I enjoy, how I like to stay active, and a simple look at how I organize my week around training and practice. Everything here is based on my own interests and experiences as a sports fan and casual player.

Scroll down to see my favourite sports, sample training schedule, and a small gallery of sports moments that inspire me.

My Favourite Sports

I enjoy watching and playing different sports for different reasons. Some are fast and intense, while others are more about strategy and patience. Here are three of my favourites.

Basketball

I like basketball because it’s fast-paced and team-oriented. I enjoy practicing layups, shooting from the three-point line, and trying to improve my ball-handling skills.

Soccer

Soccer is all about movement and timing. I like playing casually with friends and trying to improve my passing, dribbling, and overall stamina on the field.

Running / Track

Running helps me clear my mind and stay in shape. I try to mix short sprints with longer steady runs so I can work on both speed and endurance.

Sample Weekly Training Schedule

This is a simple example of how I might organize a training week around school and other responsibilities. Some days are more intense, and some are focused on rest and recovery.

Day Activity Focus Duration
Monday Basketball shooting drills Form, footwork, three-pointers 45 minutes
Tuesday Light jog or run Endurance, pacing 30 minutes
Wednesday Casual soccer with friends Teamwork, passing 1 hour
Thursday Rest or stretching Recovery, flexibility 20–30 minutes
Friday Interval sprints on track Speed, acceleration 30 minutes
Saturday Mixed sports day Fun, trying different drills 1–2 hours
Sunday Rest Recovery and planning —
